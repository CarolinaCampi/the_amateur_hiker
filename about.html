Hi! I'm Carolina, an avid traveller from Buenos Aires, Argentina.

During 2024, bacause of random turns in life, I quit my job. As an unexpected, but entirely welcome, result, my partner and I got to travel quite a bit. One year later, I find myself wanting to record some of these adventures and to share them with whoever might listen (or read). We had a few learnings along the way that might be useful to other travelers visiting these amazing places. If you are reading this, welcome and thank you for being here!
